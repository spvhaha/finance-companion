# Financial Companion — User Guide

## What is this app?
A personal financial wellness companion that gives you daily tips, tracks your savings goals, keeps a financial journal, and answers money questions using AI. Tailored for Canadian financial context (TFSA, RRSP, CPP, OAS, GICs).

---

## How to Access

### On Any Device (Phone, Tablet, Laptop)
- **URL:** https://finance-companion-two.vercel.app
- Works on any browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox
- Bookmark it or add to your phone's home screen for quick access

### Add to Phone Home Screen (like an app)
**iPhone:**
1. Open the URL in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**Android:**
1. Open the URL in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add"

## First Time Setup

1. Open the app URL
2. You'll see the **Welcome Screen** — tap "Get Started"
3. Choose your profile:
   - **Growth Explorer** — for younger investors (20s-30s), long time horizon, learning to invest
   - **Steady Protector** — for pre-retirement investors (50s-60s), focused on preserving wealth
4. Optionally enter your name (for personalized greetings)
5. Tap "Continue" — you'll land on the Dashboard

---

## Pages & How to Use Them

### 1. Dashboard (Home) — Bottom nav: ⌂
Your daily landing page. Visit every day to build your streak!

- **Greeting** — personalized by time of day and your name
- **Stats Bar** — three boxes showing:
  - 🔥 **Day Streak** — consecutive days you've visited (miss a day = resets to 1)
  - ✓ **Challenges** — total weekly challenges you've completed
  - ◎ **Active Goals** — how many savings goals are in progress
- **Daily Tip** — a new financial tip every day, specific to your profile. Same tip all day, new one tomorrow.
- **Weekly Challenge** — one actionable financial task per week with difficulty badge (Easy/Medium/Hard). Tap "Mark Complete" when done.
- **Goal Progress** — preview of your top 3 active goals with progress bars

### 2. Goals — Bottom nav: ◎
Track your savings goals.

**Create a goal:**
1. Tap "+ New Goal" (top right)
2. Type a goal name and target amount, then tap "Create Goal"
3. OR pick a quick template at the bottom (pre-filled goals for your profile)

**Templates by profile:**
- Growth Explorer: Emergency fund ($5,000), Max TFSA ($7,000), First investment ($1,000)
- Steady Protector: 2-year cash buffer ($80,000), Estate plan fund ($5,000), Travel fund ($10,000)

**Update progress:**
- Tap **+$50**, **+$100**, or **+$500** buttons on any goal
- When you reach 100%, you get a 🎉 celebration animation!

**Delete a goal:**
- Tap the **✕** in the top-right corner of the goal card

### 3. Journal — Bottom nav: ✎
Your private financial diary.

- A **writing prompt** appears at the top (changes daily) for inspiration
- Write your thoughts in the text box
- Tap **"Save Entry"**
- All entries appear below, newest first
- Tap **Edit** to modify or **Delete** to remove any entry

**Example prompts:**
- "What money decision did you make today?"
- "What did you learn about finance this week?"
- "What financial worry keeps you up at night?"

### 4. Ask Me — Bottom nav: ?
AI-powered financial Q&A using Claude AI.

- Tap a **suggestion chip** (pre-written question) OR type your own
- Tap **"Ask"** and wait a few seconds
- AI response appears in a card below
- Conversation history is saved — you can scroll down to see past Q&As
- Follow-up questions work (the AI remembers recent context)

**Example questions:**
- "Should I pay off debt or invest?"
- "How does a TFSA work?"
- "When should I take CPP?"
- "What's a safe withdrawal rate?"

**Note:** Responses are general financial education, not professional advice.

### 5. Settings — Bottom nav: ⚙
Manage your account and data.

- **Profile** — see your current profile type, tap "Switch" to change
- **Display Name** — update your name, tap "Save"
- **Statistics** — all your stats: streak, challenges done, goals created, journal entries
- **Export Data** — downloads everything as a JSON file (good for backups)
- **Reset All Data** — wipes everything clean. Requires confirmation. Cannot be undone!

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of It

1. **Visit daily** — even for 30 seconds. Read the tip, check your streak.
2. **Complete weekly challenges** — small real-world actions build financial habits.
3. **Update goals when you actually save money** — watching progress bars grow is motivating.
4. **Journal at least once a week** — writing about money decisions builds self-awareness.
5. **Use Ask Me when curious** — no question is too basic. The AI is friendly and non-judgmental.
6. **Export your data periodically** — Settings → Export gives you a backup JSON file.
